Indonesian Language Learning Program

We provide two options which are Short Term and Regular, longer term Program.

Basic Program

The program is aimed at those who would like to visit Indonesia for a short trip but would like to know just a little useful words and common daily phrases.

Regular Program

This program consists of 4 levels. Each level will explore a more in depth formation of words and sentences in Indonesian. We aim to emphasize on comprehension of proper grammatical use of words in sentences so that the language is understood and used correctly.
